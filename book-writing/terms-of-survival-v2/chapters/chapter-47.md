# Chapter 47: The Letter

The thing about letters is that they're personal.

The thing about personal is that it matters.

The thing about matter is that it's heavy.

---

A letter arrived.

From the President. Thanking me for my service. Offering a medal.

I threw it away.

"That's rude," Sara said.

"It's honest."

"She was trying to help."

"I don't need help."

"You never do."

She was right.
