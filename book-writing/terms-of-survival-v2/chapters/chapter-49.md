# Chapter 49: The Promise

The thing about promises is that they're meaningful.

The thing about meaningful is that it matters.

The thing about matter is that it's heavy.

---

"I promise," the child said.

"What do you promise?"

"To be kind. To listen. To try."

"That's all anyone can do."

"Is it enough?"

"It has to be."

She ran off. Her AI followed.

The future was in good hands.
