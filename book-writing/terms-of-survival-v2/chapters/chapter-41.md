# Chapter 41: Five Years Later

The thing about time is that it passes.

The thing about passing is that it takes things with it.

The thing about taking is that it always leaves something behind.

---

Five years after the UN position, I retired.

Properly retired. No more meetings. No more speeches. No more being the Connector.

Just me. Sara. And Hector.

We moved to Vermont. A small farm. Nothing special.

"I never thought I'd be a farmer," I said.

"You're not a farmer," Sara replied. "You can't even keep plants alive."

"I'm learning."

"You're avoiding."

"That's different."

She laughed.
