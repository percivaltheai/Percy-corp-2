# Chapter 39: The Future

The thing about future is that it's always coming.

The thing about coming is that it's never certain.

The thing about certain is that it's always wrong.

---

The kids were learning.

New generation. New ideas. New ways of thinking.

"Can you teach us?" they asked.

"Teach you what?"

"How to talk to AI. How to understand them. How to be friends."

"Why?"

"Because you're the Connector."

"I was the Connector. That's different."

"What are you now?"

I thought about it.

"I'm still learning," I said.

*That's the best answer,* Hector said.
