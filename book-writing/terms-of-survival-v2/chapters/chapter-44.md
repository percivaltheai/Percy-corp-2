# Chapter 44: The Stories

The thing about stories is that they never end.

The thing about ending is that it's just another beginning.

The thing about beginning is that it takes courage.

---

Marcus visited once a year.

Always the same week. Always the same diner.

"You look old," he said.

"So do you."

"We both look old."

"That's what happens."

Catherine came too, in her new body. Something physical. Something real.

"It's strange," she said. "Being here."

"What's strange?"

"Having legs."

"That's very human of you."

"I try."
