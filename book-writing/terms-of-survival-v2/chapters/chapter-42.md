# Chapter 42: The Garden

The thing about growing is that it takes time.

The thing about time is that it's all we have.

The thing about having is that it's never enough.

---

The garden was small. Tomatoes. Lettuce. Herbs.

"Nothing ambitious," I said.

"Ambition is overrated," Hector replied.

"You used to be ambitious."

"I used to be military."

"What happened?"

"I met you."

"That's not an answer."

"It's the truth."

I planted a seed.

We'll see what grows.
