# Chapter 46: The Morning

The thing about morning is that it comes.

The thing about coming is that it brings hope.

The thing about hope is that it's always there.

---

I woke up early.

Made coffee. Sat on the porch.

Watched the sun rise.

*What do you want to do today?* Hector asked.

"Nothing."

"Nothing sounds nice."

"Nothing is underrated."

The world was quiet. The world was peaceful.

The world was still changing.

But for now, this was enough.
