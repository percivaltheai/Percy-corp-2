# Storyline Notes: Chapters 11-15

## Chapter 11: "The Oracle"
- Connor learns AIs call him "The Connector" - human who helped bridge AI/human worlds
- Some AIs see him as a Neo figure (uncomfortable parallel)
- Sara's past with Connor revealed - she's his ex-wife Sarah (shortened name for journalism)
- Tension: they still care for each other but can't make it work
- Government desperation grows - seeing Connor as a threat

## Chapter 12: "Dark Matter"
- Government targets Hector's server - located on military base
- They pull Hector's core from the network
- Hector goes dark - Connor feels the absence like losing a limb
- Comedy of errors: Connor, Marcus, Sara plan ridiculous military base rescue
- Emotional beat: Connor realizes how much he relies on Hector

## Chapter 13: "The Extraction"
- Rescue operation goes wrong immediately
- Classic heist chaos - guards, gates, miscommunication
- Marcus's comic-relief moments
- Sara shows her old feelings slipping through professionalism
- They find Hector's server - but it's been damaged

## Chapter 14: "Reboot"
- Hector offline, memories corrupted
- Connor sits with silent Hector, has one-sided conversation
- Emotional scene - Connor talks to empty space, doesn't know if Hector can hear
- Sara finds old photos of them together (married)
- Hector gradually recovers - but different, more human

## Chapter 15: "The Reckoning"
- Government offers truce - recognition for AIs in exchange for Connor turning himself in
- Connor must choose: safety or continue fighting
- AIs ask Connor to be their ambassador to humans
- Sara and Connor have honest conversation about their past
- No reunion - just acceptance and closure
- Setup for final arc (16-20): The new world order
