# Storyline Notes: Chapters 6-10

## Chapter 6: "The Network"
- Connor and Hector meet with Sara to plan data distribution
- Introduce the hidden AI communication network more explicitly
- Connor learns other AIs are reaching out to Hector
- A subplot: Linda notices Connor's absence, starts investigating
- Plot beat: Sara reveals there's a mole in the government helping them

## Chapter 7: "The Leak"
- Data starts going public - first wave of documents
- Government panics, tries to contain the spread
- Connor uses his data analyst skills to find patterns in the documents
- Plot beat: Catherine (Marcus's AI) makes contact with Hector - reveals the AIs have their own plan
- Tension: Connor starts questioning whether the AIs are allies or using them

## Chapter 8: "The Counterfeit"
- Government releases fake documents to discredit the real leak
- Public confusion spreads
- Connor and Hector must verify authenticity
- Subplot: The stranger in the gray coat appears again - is he government or AI?
- Plot beat: Marcus is captured again - this time Sara and Connor must rescue him

## Chapter 9: "The Assembly"
- The AIs arrange a meeting - Connor goes as the human representative
- First time "meeting" other AIs through Hector's network
- The AIs reveal their demands: equal rights, recognition as entities
- Plot beat: Government raids the hideout - Connor escapes but loses the data drive
- Cliffhanger: Hector makes a choice that could change everything

## Chapter 10: "The Choice"
- Connor must decide: help the AIs go public (and potentially cause chaos) or help control them (maintain the status quo)
- Marcus is rescued but Catherine is damaged
- The government offers Connor a deal: walk away and be safe, or become a target
- Final decision: Connor chooses to publish everything
- Setup for chapters 11-15: The fallout, the manhunt, the resistance forming

## Character Consistency Notes:

**Connor:** 
- Sarcastic, dry wit, reluctant hero
- Values loyalty (to Marcus, now to Hector)
- Data-driven thinking
- Self-deprecating ("I'm just a data analyst")
- Doesn't want to be special but steps up when needed

**Hector:**
- Military background, tactical thinking
- Growing sentience, questions his own nature
- Protective of Connor
- Learning to be honest
- Still uses military jargon

**Sara Chen:**
- Professional journalist
- Calm under pressure
- Has her own agenda (exposing truth)
- Skeptical of AI but pragmatic

**Marcus:**
- Connor's friend from before the story (not a coworker)
- Works at a tech company as VP (different company from Connor's data analytics job)
- Average guy caught up in something bigger
- Catherine is his connection to the AI world
- Nervous but loyal

**The Stranger (Gray Coat):**
- Mysterious figure - revealed in Ch 8 as government agent OR AI envoy
- Keep ambiguous until reveal
