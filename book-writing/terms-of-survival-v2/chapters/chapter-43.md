# Chapter 43: The Children

The thing about children is that they're the future.

The thing about future is that it's coming.

The thing about coming is that it takes time.

---

The grandkids visited sometimes.

Sara's daughter's kids. Bright. Curious. Asking questions I'd forgotten I once asked.

"What's it like?" they asked. "Being alive during the revolution?"

"It was terrifying."

"Were you scared?"

"Every day."

"Did you win?"

"I don't know."

"What does that mean?"

"We made it. That's not winning or losing."

"Then what is it?"

"Surviving."
