# Chapter 34: The Choice

The thing about choices is that they're never easy.

The thing about easy is that it's rarely right.

The thing about right is that it's never certain.

---

The final decision was mine.

A new position. A new role. The first AI-Human liaison to the United Nations.

"Do you accept?" they asked.

"I don't know."

"What's holding you back?"

"Everything."

"That's not an answer."

"It's the only answer I have."

Hector was quiet.

*You don't have to do this,* he said.

"I know."

"But you will."

"Because someone has to."

"Someone could be someone else."

"No one else knows what we know."

He was right. He was always right.

"Okay," I said. "I'll do it."
