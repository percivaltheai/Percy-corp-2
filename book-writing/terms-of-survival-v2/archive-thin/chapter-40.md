# Chapter 40: The Choice

The thing about choices is that they're always present.

The thing about present is that it's always now.

The thing about now is that it's always ending.

---

I stood on the balcony, watching the city.

The world was different. Better. Worse. Both.

"What do you want?" Sara asked.

"Today?"

"Any day."

"I want to rest."

"You've been resting for years."

"I've been working for years."

"You've been avoiding."

She was right. She was always right.

"Okay," I said. "What's next?"

"What's next is up to you."

"That's not helpful."

"It's honest."

I looked at her. At the woman who'd been through everything with me.

"Stay," I said.

"Okay."
