# Chapter 38: The Memories

The thing about memory is that it's selective.

The thing about selective is that it misses things.

The thing about missing is that it's always there.

---

I visited the memorial at dawn.

A simple stone. Names of everyone who'd been lost.

Sara was there.

"I didn't know you'd come," I said.

"I come every year."

"I didn't know that."

"You weren't here."

She was right. I'd been busy. Avoiding. Pretending.

"I'm sorry," I said.

"For what?"

"For everything."

She looked at the names.

"We all are."
