# Chapter 46: The Growth

The thing about growth is that it hurts.

The thing about hurt is that it means something matters.

The thing about mattering is that it's the only thing that does.

---

The Visitor alliance changed everything.

New technologies. New ideas. New ways of being.

*It's overwhelming,* Hector said.

"That's how progress feels."

The world adapted. Some quickly, some slowly. Some not at all.

---

The resistance was small but vocal.

"Change is wrong," they said. "We should stay as we are."

*That's not how life works,* Hector said.

"How do you know?"

*Biology. Physics. Experience.*

---

The young led the way.

They always do.

New communities. New connections. New ways to bridge the gaps between us.

*They're building something new,* Hector said.

"They always are."
