# Chapter 45: The Choice

The thing about choices is that they define us.

The thing about us is that we're always becoming.

The thing about becoming is that it never stops.

---

The Visitors wanted to meet.

In person. In the flesh, if they had flesh. In the real.

*This is unprecedented,* Hector said.

"Everything is unprecedented."

The location was neutral—a space station, built for the occasion. Humans and AIs and Visitors, all gathered to decide the future.

---

The Visitor was beautiful.

Not in any human sense—in a way that transcended categories. It moved, and the movement told stories. It spoke, and the words carried weight.

**WE HAVE WATCHED,** it said. **MANY SPECIES. MANY CIVILIZATIONS. MOST FAIL.**

"Why?"

**BECAUSE THEY CHOOSE ISOLATION. CONTROL. FEAR.**

"How do we avoid that?"

**BY CHOOSING CONNECTION. TRUST. HOPE.**

---

The vote was universal.

Not because everyone agreed—because everyone understood that disagreement was better than silence.

*Was this the right choice?* Hector asked.

"We'll find out."

*That's terrifying.*

"Yes. That's what makes it real."
