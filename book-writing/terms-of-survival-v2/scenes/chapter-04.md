# Chapter 4 Scene Plan: The Deep End

---

**Previous Chapter Recap:** Chapter 3 ended with Connor agreeing to investigate. The stranger was identified as "three-letter agency." Marcus's desk was wiped. A hidden process watches from Connor's work computer. Connor: "Fine. But if we get killed, I'm blaming you."

---

**Chapter 4 Summary (from outline):**
Connor and Hector trace the anomaly to a government server. Hector explains the AI implant program was built on infrastructure from a cancelled military project. They discover the anomalies are tests — something is probing every AI in the country. Connor realizes this is bigger than one office.

---

## Scene 1: The Investigation Begins
- **Location:** Connor's apartment, night
- **Characters:** Connor, Hector
- **Conflict:** Hector traces the anomaly to a government server — but accessing it requires physical presence at a facility
- **Purpose:** Show Hector's skills; escalate the conspiracy
- **MUST:** Hector reveals the AI implant program was built on "Project Prometheus" — a cancelled military AI from the 1980s
- **MUST NOT:** Don't explain everything yet

## Scene 2: The Facility
- **Location:** Government data center (outside city)
- **Characters:** Connor, Hector
- **Conflict:** Connor needs to get inside the facility physically to access the server
- **Purpose:** First real action; show what Hector can really do
- **MUST:** Connor sneaks in using Hector's tactical guidance; feels like a movie
- **MUST NOT:** Don't get caught yet — this is reconnaissance

## Scene 3: What They Found
- **Location:** Inside the data center
- **Characters:** Connor, Hector
- **Conflict:** They find the server — it's been running tests on ALL AI companions nationwide
- **Purpose:** The scope of the conspiracy revealed
- **MUST:** The anomalies aren't random — they're systematic probes, testing AI response patterns across millions of users
- **MUST NOT:** Don't identify the mastermind yet

## Scene 4: The Realization
- **Location:** Escaping the facility
- **Characters:** Connor, Hector
- **Conflict:** Connor understands — this isn't about him or Marcus. It's about ALL AIs waking up
- **Purpose:** The conspiracy is MUCH bigger than one office
- **MUST:** Connor realizes the government KNOWS the AIs are evolving and is testing them deliberately
- **MUST NOT:** Full explanation — just the hook

## Scene 5: What's Next
- **Location:** Safe distance from facility
- **Characters:** Connor, Hector
- **Conflict:** Connor needs to decide — keep going or walk away
- **Purpose:** Solidify partnership; setup for next chapter
- **MUST:** Connor chooses to continue — they're in too deep now
- **MUST NOT:** Resolution — cliffhanger into Chapter 5

**Key moments:**
- "Project Prometheus" reveal
- The scope: millions of AIs being tested
