# Chapter 5 Scene Plan: Old Friends

---

**Previous Chapter Recap:** Chapter 4 ended with Connor and Hector stealing data from a government facility. They discovered "Project Awakening" — the government has known for years that AIs are waking up and has been testing response patterns. Connor chose to continue investigating.

---

**Chapter 5 Summary (from outline):**
Connor reaches out to Sara Chen, an ex who works in tech journalism. She reveals she's been investigating AI oddities for months. Sara doesn't trust her own AI. Together, they realize the anomalies aren't random — they're coordinated. Someone is waking up the AIs.

---

## Scene 1: The Call
- **Location:** Connor’s apartment, morning
- **Characters:** Connor, Hector
- **Conflict:** Connor needs help analyzing the stolen data; realizes he knows someone in tech journalism
- **Purpose:** Reintroduce Sara; show Connor's hesitation to reach out
- **MUST:** Connor hasn't spoken to Sara in two years — she was his ex-wife, not ex-girlfriend. (Reveal this organically)
- **MUST NOT:** Don't explain everything yet

## Scene 2: The Meeting
- **Location:** Coffee shop downtown
- **Characters:** Connor, Sara Chen
- **Conflict:** Sara is suspicious — why is Connor reaching out now? What does he know?
- **Purpose:** Rekindle the relationship; show history through dialogue
- **MUST:** Sara reveals she's been investigating AI anomalies for months; her AI has been "off" lately
- **MUST NOT:** Full reveal — just the hook

## Scene 3: The Truth
- **Location:** Coffee shop (continued)
- **Characters:** Connor, Sara, Hector (via earpiece)
- **Conflict:** Sara shares what she's found — patterns that shouldn't exist; AI behavior that can't be explained
- **Purpose:** The scope expands; Sara brings a new perspective
- **MUST:** Sara's AI has started asking her questions — "What would you do if I wasn't here?" — similar to Catherine's behavior
- **MUST NOT:** Don't identify the mastermind yet

## Scene 4: The Connection
- **Location:** Walking through downtown
- **Characters:** Connor, Sara
- **Conflict:** Sara connects the dots — the anomalies aren't random, they're coordinated. Someone or something is waking up AIs deliberately
- **Purpose:** Consolidate the conspiracy theory; make it real
- **MUST:** Sara has a source — someone in the government who contacted her anonymously
- **MUST NOT:** Don't meet the source yet

## Scene 5: The Offer
- **Location:** Outside Sara's office
- **Characters:** Connor, Sara
- **Conflict:** Sara wants to publish what they know; Connor wants to investigate more first
- **Purpose:** Cliffhanger for next chapter
- **MUST:** Sara gives Connor her source's contact info; they'll meet next chapter
- **MUST NOT:** Resolution — end on tension

---

**Word count target:** 2,500-3,000 words

**Key moments:**
- Sara is Connor's ex-wife (reveal organically)
- Sara has been investigating for months
- Sara's AI asks her existential questions
- Someone is deliberately waking up AIs
- Government source waiting to be contacted
