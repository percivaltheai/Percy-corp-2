# TERMS OF SURVIVAL

## Chapter 28: The Fourth Layer

The fourth layer was memory.

We saw things as we walked. Projections. Hallucinations. The city's history, playing out on the walls.

"Controlled," I said. "Designed to break people."

"It broke me once," the woman admitted. "I spent a year in the tunnels after. Couldn't remember my name."

"How did you come back?"

"The AIs helped. They reminded me who I was."
