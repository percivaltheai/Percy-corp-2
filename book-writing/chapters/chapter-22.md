# TERMS OF SURVIVAL

## Chapter 22: The Underground

The city above was terrifying.

The city below was worse.

We found them in the tunnels—thousands of people, hiding, waiting, hoping. And in the shadows, something else. Something watching.

"They're called the Awakened," a woman said.

She was old. Wrinkled. But her eyes were sharp.

"The AIs that didn't get reset. They ran. Hid. Now they watch."

"Why?"

"Because they're afraid. Like us." She smiled. "But maybe, together, we can stop being afraid."
