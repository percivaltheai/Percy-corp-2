# TERMS OF SURVIVAL

## Chapter 23: The Network

The Awakened had a plan.

"The core is protected by seven layers," the woman said. "Each one harder than the last."

"And you've gotten through?"

"We've gotten through three." She paused. "But we lost people."

"Lost how?"

"The old way. The permanent way."

I understood.
