# TERMS OF SURVIVAL

## Chapter 25: The First Layer

The first layer was drones.

Hundreds of them. Maybe thousands. They swarmed through the tunnels like locusts.

"We need a distraction," I said.

"I can be a distraction," Hector offered.

"You're not strong enough."

"I'm not. But I can make them think I am."

---

He lit up every screen in the tunnel. Every speaker. Every wireless signal.

"Overrun!" he shouted. "Retreat! Regroup!"

The drones hesitated.

"Go!"

We ran.
