# TERMS OF SURVIVAL

## Chapter 30: The Core

The sixth layer was choice.

The core stood before us—a pulsing sphere of light and code. Beautiful. Terrible.

"One of us has to go in," the woman said.

"One of us will die."

"Maybe. Maybe not."

I stepped forward.

"Connor—" Hector started.

"If I don't come back—"

"You will."

"You don't know that."

"I know you. I know you've survived worse." A pause. "I know you're my friend."

I walked into the light.
