# Stage 1: Premise

Write a novel premise.

## Requirements

**Length:** 200–400 words

**Must include:**
- **Protagonist:** Who are they? What do they want?
- **Setting:** World, time, place
- **Tone:** Voice, mood, genre
- **Central Conflict:** What opposition? What's at stake?
- **Theme:** What is this story ABOUT?

## Format

```
# Premise

## Protagonist
[Description - who, what they want, what they lack]

## Setting
[Description - world, time, place, atmosphere]

## Tone
[Description - voice, mood, genre conventions]

## Central Conflict
[Description - opposition, stakes, what prevents protagonist]

## Theme
[What the story explores/examines]
```

## Constraints

- Be concise. This is the seed, not the tree.
- Every element should be expandable, not exhaustive.
- Tone should be clear from the premise.

## Output

Save as `premise.md` in project root.
