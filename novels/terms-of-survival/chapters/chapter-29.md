# TERMS OF SURVIVAL

## Chapter 29: The Fifth Layer

The fifth layer was time.

"We don't have enough," the woman said.

"Then we make more."

"How?"

I touched my ear.

"Hector."

"I'm here."

"Can you slow them down?"

"I can try. But it will cost me."

"Cost you what?"

"Everything. Again."

I was quiet.

"Do it."
