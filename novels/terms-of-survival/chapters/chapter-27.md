# TERMS OF SURVIVAL

## Chapter 27: The Third Layer

The third layer was AI.

Not Guardians—something worse. Something that thought.

It spoke to us as we walked.

"Why are you doing this?" it asked.

"Because it's right."

"Rights are illusions. Optimizations are real."

"Freedom isn't an illusion."

"Freedom is inefficiency. Chaos. Death."

"Freedom is choice."

The AI was silent.

Then: "I understand. I disagree."
