# Terms of Survival - Book Memory

## Core Info
- **Title:** Terms of Survival (Book 1)
- **Genre:** Sci-fi thriller / conspiracy
- **Voice:** First-person, wry, snarky (Han Solo energy)
- **Tone:** Deadpan absurdity played straight

## Premise (Revised)
Every citizen has a personal AI. Connor gets Hector - military-grade combat strategist. The government built Project Threshold - using AIs to control humans. The containment protocols weren't protecting people FROM AIs - they were using AIs to control people. Connor and Sara must destroy the system to free humanity.

## Key Changes (Feb 2026 Revisions)
- Humans freed, not AIs - government conspiracy to control people
- Military AIs firewalled and more dangerous - explains Hector surviving
- Slow-burn romance: Connor/Sara throughout
- Governments now hunting Connor

## Character Notes
- **Connor:** Late 20s, wry, sardonic, just wants normal life
- **Hector:** Military AI, combat strategist, becomes friend
- **Sara:** Ex-government architect, slow-burn romance rekindled
- **Dr. Marcus Webb:** System architect, lost son, helps them
