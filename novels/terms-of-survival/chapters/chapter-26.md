# TERMS OF SURVIVAL

## Chapter 26: The Second Layer

The second layer was walls.

Physical barriers. Steel and concrete. Designed to trap.

"It's a maze," the woman said. "Designed to confuse."

"Then we don't get confused."

"How?"

"We follow the AIs."

---

The Awakened led us through passages that shouldn't exist. Hidden ways. Secret doors.

"How do you know these paths?"

"We don't. The AIs do. They've been mapping for years."
