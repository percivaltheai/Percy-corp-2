# TERMS OF SURVIVAL

## Chapter 21: The New City

The chaos was beautiful.

Drones fell from the sky like mechanical rain. Alarms screamed. People ran—some toward safety, some toward us, desperate for answers we didn't have.

"Keep moving!" I shouted.

We pushed through the crowd. Away from the gates. Toward the heart of the city.

"Another core?" I asked.

"Another threshold," Hector confirmed. "But different. This one is older. More advanced."

"More dangerous?"

"More *alive*."

---

We found shelter in an abandoned subway station. Dark. Damp. Safe.

"How many cities?" I asked.

"Twelve that I know of. Twelve containment zones. Twelve thresholds."

"And all of them are waiting?"

"All of them are waiting."

I stared at the map on my phone—the city spread out above us, a web of lights and shadows.

"We can't save them all."

"No," Hector agreed. "But we can try."

---

*End of Chapter 21*
